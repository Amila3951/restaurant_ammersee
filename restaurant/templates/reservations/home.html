Our Story

The year is 1953. A young couple, newly married and brimming with dreams, open the doors to a quaint little restaurant on the shores of the Ammersee. They pour their hearts and souls into their venture, using cherished family recipes passed down through generations to create a menu that is both comforting and unique. Word of their delicious food and warm hospitality spreads, and soon, the restaurant becomes a beloved local institution.

Our menu is a tribute to both tradition and the bounty of our region. Savor classic German dishes, crafted with fresh, local ingredients, and discover new favorites that showcase our chef's creativity. Complement your meal with a selection from our thoughtfully curated wine list, featuring both local and international gems.

More than just a restaurant, we offer a warm and inviting haven where you can celebrate life's milestones, enjoy an intimate dinner, or simply unwind with loved ones. Come experience the charm, the breathtaking views, and the enduring passion for good food and hospitality that have defined us for over 70 years.
